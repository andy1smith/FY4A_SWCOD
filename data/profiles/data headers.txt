"aerosol_profile.csv" from yu2010, CALIPSO lidar measurements
Col0: height [m]
col1: all season profile relative to surface AOD
col2: spring profile relative to surface AOD
col3: summer profile relative to surface AOD
col4: fall profile relative to surface AOD
col5: winter profile relative to surface AOD

"aerosol_refraction.csv" from lubin2002
col0: wavelength [um]
col1: real part of refraction index
col2: imaginary part of refraction index

"AFGL *.csv" see 'Data references/AFGL profiles.xlsx'

"* Contm *.csv" from MT_CKD continuum Fortran code
col0: wavenumber [cm-1]
colx: coefficients

"emissivity of passive cooling material.csv" from Zhai2017 Science
col0: wavelength [um]
col1: wavenumber [cm-1]
col2: emittance of the material

"Reflectance of CSP.txt" from Good2016, Data in Brief
col0: wavelength [nm]
col1: reflectance when indident at 15 deg
col2: reflectance when indident at 45 deg
col3: reflectance when indident at 60 deg

"Reflectance of PV.txt" from Patel2017, Data in Brief
col0: wavelength [nm]
col1: reflectance 

"SolarTOA.csv" from AM0AM1_5.xls
col0: wavelength [um]
col1: flux density [W/m2/um]

"water_refraction.csv" from hale1973optical
col0: wavelength [um]
col1: real part of refraction index
